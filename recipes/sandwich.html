ham and chese sandwich.

A quick and easy recipe for a basic ham and chese sandwich

Best when you want something fast and tasty

ingredients

  • white sandwich bread
  • mayo
  • ham
  • chese
  • veggies(optional)

steps

  1. grab two pieces of bread and place them on counter
  2. grab a butter knife and gently spread the mayo on both slices
  3. add a generous amount of ham on one side of the bread
  4. place a couple slices of cheese
  5. place second bread on top
  6. enjoy
